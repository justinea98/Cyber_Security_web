Basic Cyber Security Questions

What is Cyber Security?:
Cyber Security is the protection of information that out into cyber space. It's meant to protect networks, programs, and other types of data from attacks, damages or other types of foreign viruses. The protection of information is very critical to everyone who has any type of private information that is stored in the internet.
